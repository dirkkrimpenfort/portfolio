Past


Hi, I'm Dirk. When I got my first ATARI ST at the age of 20, I knew immediately that computers were my passion. I was very proud of my first basic program, the output of which appeared on my old tube television in green letters on a black background. So much for the beginnings. My passion developed over the years into my own IT business, which I ran for almost three decades. During this time I learned a lot about computer hardware, networks, operating systems, databases and office programs. A few years ago I decided to close my business to look for new challenges.
